# Ingrown Nail Removal

Ingrown nails can be painful. Our team provides professional ingrown nail care:

**Nursing Care:**
- Ingrown nail cleanup and management
- Regular nail care to prevent ingrown nails
- Assessment and monitoring

**Medical Procedure:**
If needed, Dr. Nesbitt can perform ingrown nail removal procedures at the clinic. The doctor is available on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

For persistent or severely ingrown nails, we recommend booking when Dr. Nesbitt is available for same-day assessment and treatment.
