# Hammertoes

Hammertoe is a deformity of the second, third, or fourth toe causing it to bend at the middle joint, resembling a hammer. Our team provides assessment and management.

**What We Offer:**
- Assessment of toe alignment and flexibility
- Footwear advice
- Orthotic assessment for offloading pressure
- Padding and protection for affected areas
- Referral for medical evaluation if needed

Proper fitting footwear and orthotic solutions can help manage symptoms and prevent progression.
