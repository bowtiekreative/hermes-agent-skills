# Information for Referring Physicians

Calgary Foot Care Nurses provides comprehensive foot care services that complement physician-managed care. We welcome referrals from physicians, nurse practitioners, and other healthcare providers.

**Services We Provide for Your Patients:**
- Medical foot care (nail care, callus/corn removal)
- Diabetic foot monitoring and education
- Neuropathy assessment and management
- PVD assessment
- Ulcer care and wound management
- Compression therapy
- Orthotic assessment (through Onward Orthotics)
- Mobile services for homebound patients

**Dr. John Nesbitt** provides on-site medical oversight and is available Tuesdays and Thursdays for:
- Biomechanical assessments
- Ulcer management
- Out-patient surgical procedures
- Comprehensive foot and ankle care
