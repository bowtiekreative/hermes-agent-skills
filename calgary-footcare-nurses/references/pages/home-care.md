# Home Care

Our home care services bring professional medical foot care to patients who cannot easily travel to our clinic. We serve patients in their homes, senior residences, and care facilities.

**Services Available at Home:**
- Medical foot care (nail care, callus/corn removal)
- Leg and foot assessments
- Regular maintenance visits (recommended every 6-8 weeks)
- Diabetic foot monitoring
- Education on foot health

**Mobile Hours:** Monday to Friday, 8:00am – 4:00pm
