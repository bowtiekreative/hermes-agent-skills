# About Us

Calgary Foot Care Nurses was founded to address a need for **Medical Foot Care**, especially during the pandemic when many patients could not leave home. The company offers safe, convenient foot care services both in-clinic and via mobile services.

## Mission

**To restore function and quality of life.**

We are committed to compassionate, patient-centered, high-quality care. We use our office staff efficiently to offer exceptional, accessible, personal care in a comfortable atmosphere.

## Values

- **Quality & Support:** Provide services in a caring, supportive environment with integrity, respect, and confidentiality
- **Efficiency & Effectiveness:** Organize resources to treat patients efficiently; strive to exceed expectations by improving access and quality
- **Collaboration:** Work together for patients; value the essential role of the doctor; create processes to help him serve patients; value staff as important resources
