# Education & Foot Health Awareness

We are committed to improving health awareness and literacy amongst our patients. Education is a key part of every appointment.

**Topics We Cover:**
- Proper foot care techniques
- Diabetic foot self-care
- Neuropathy awareness and management
- Proper footwear selection
- Signs and symptoms to watch for
- When to seek medical attention
- Compression therapy education
- Skin and nail care best practices

We believe that informed patients make better decisions about their health. Every appointment includes an educational component tailored to your specific needs and conditions.
