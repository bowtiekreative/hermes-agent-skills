# Orthotic Prescriptions

We partner with **Onward Orthotics** and **Heather Orosz**, Canadian Certified Pedorthist, to provide comprehensive orthotic solutions.

**What We Offer:**
- Biomechanical assessment
- Custom orthotic creation and modification
- Footwear fitting and recommendations
- Treatment for a wide range of conditions

**Conditions Treated with Orthotics:**
- Plantar fasciitis and heel spurs
- Flat feet/fallen arches
- Arch pain
- Bunions
- Neuromas
- Metatarsalgia/forefoot pain
- Structural abnormalities
- Sports injuries
- Achilles tendonitis
- Shin splints
- Knee pain
- Back pain

**Heather Orosz** has a Bachelor of Science in Biomechanics and has been a Certified Pedorthist since 2005, treating everything from athletic injuries to high-risk diabetic feet.
