# Leg & Foot Assessment

A comprehensive leg and foot assessment is the foundation of good foot health. We offer assessments both in-clinic and through mobile services.

**What an Assessment Includes:**
- Lower extremity examination
- Skin and nail assessment
- Circulatory assessment (including ABI when indicated)
- Neurological screening
- Biomechanical evaluation
- Gait assessment (when applicable)
- Orthotic assessment (with referral to pedorthist)

**Who Should Get Assessed:**
- Diabetic patients
- Those with neuropathy or PVD
- Patients with chronic foot pain
- Seniors requiring regular foot care
- Anyone concerned about their foot health

Assessments can be done in-clinic or through our mobile service.
