# Bunions

A bunion is a bony bump that forms on the joint at the base of your big toe. Our team can help assess and manage bunions.

**Our Approach:**
- Assessment of bunion severity and impact
- Footwear recommendations
- Orthotic assessment through Onward Orthotics
- Pain management strategies
- Referral to Dr. Nesbitt for medical evaluation

**Symptoms We Help Manage:**
- Pain or soreness at the base of the big toe
- Swelling, redness, or inflammation
- Difficulty wearing certain shoes
- Corns or calluses on the affected foot
