# Ingrown Nail Care

We provide comprehensive care for ingrown toenails, from regular maintenance to medical intervention.

**Nursing Care:**
- Professional nail trimming and cleanup
- Ingrown nail prevention
- Education on proper nail cutting technique
- Regular monitoring

**Medical Treatment:**
Dr. Nesbitt can perform ingrown nail removal procedures at the clinic on Tuesdays and Thursdays for persistent or severe cases.

**Prevention Tips:**
- Cut nails straight across, not curved
- Wear properly fitting shoes
- Avoid cutting nails too short
- Regular professional foot care (every 6-8 weeks)
