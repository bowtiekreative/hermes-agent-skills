# Diabetic Foot Care

Diabetes can affect your feet in many ways. Our team specializes in diabetic foot care, providing regular monitoring, neuropathy assessment, and wound prevention strategies.

**Our Services for Diabetic Patients:**
- Regular foot assessments and monitoring
- Neuropathy screening
- Peripheral vascular assessment
- Skin and nail care
- Ulcer prevention and management
- Education on diabetic foot self-care
- Coordination with Dr. Nesbitt for comprehensive care

**Why Regular Foot Care Matters for Diabetics:**
Diabetes can cause reduced blood flow and nerve damage in the feet, making regular professional foot care essential for preventing complications.

**Recommended:** Foot care every 6-8 weeks, with regular check-ups.
