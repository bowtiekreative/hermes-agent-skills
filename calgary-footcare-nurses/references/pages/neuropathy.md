# Neuropathy

Neuropathy affects the peripheral nervous system and can cause pain, numbness, and other symptoms in the feet. Our team specializes in assessing and managing neuropathic conditions.

**Our Approach:**
- Comprehensive neuropathy assessment
- Sensory testing and evaluation
- Coordination with Dr. Nesbitt for medical management
- Regular foot monitoring and care
- Education on neuropathy self-management
- Prevention of complications

Regular foot care is especially important for neuropathy patients who may not feel injuries or developing problems due to reduced sensation.
