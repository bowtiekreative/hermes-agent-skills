# Ulcer Care

Dr. John Nesbitt specializes in the management and treatment of various types of ulcers. With over 25 years of experience and 40,000+ patients treated, our team provides expert wound care.

**Types of Ulcers Treated:**
- Venous ulcers
- Diabetic ulcers
- Arterial ulcers
- Post-traumatic ulcers

**Treatment Approaches:**
- Comprehensive wound assessment
- Compressive therapy for venous stasis ulcers
- Management of symptomatic venous congestion
- Treatment of refractory non-CHF peripheral edema
- Lymphoedema management
- Ankle brachial index (ABI) assessments
- Pressure garment assessments
- On-site practical nurse high-risk skin and nail care

**Partners:** We work with NanoTess, a social enterprise using nanotechnology innovation for chronic wound care, including diabetic foot ulcers.
