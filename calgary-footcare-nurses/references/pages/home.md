# Calgary Foot Care Nurses

**Your feet deserve expert care.** We specialize in diagnosing and treating conditions related to the peripheral nervous system, including Neuropathy, Peripheral Vascular Disease, Diabetes, calluses, corns, and ingrown nails.

We offer **in-clinic care** and **mobile services** to all of our patients. With our doctor and our team of foot care nurses, we can help provide care for your foot care concerns.

> "We are offering direct billing... if you have Alberta Blue Cross we can bill directly, and you won't have to pay out of pocket."

> "The doctor is covered by Alberta Health care."

> "We suggest Foot Care done every 6-8 weeks apart."

> "Dr. Nesbitt is working on Tuesdays and Thursdays so you will have your medical Foot Care done by one of the wonderful nurses and have a check-up done by the doctor."

## Why Choose Us

- Qualified team of foot care nurses
- Doctor on-site (Tuesdays & Thursdays)
- In-clinic and mobile services
- Direct billing for Alberta Blue Cross
