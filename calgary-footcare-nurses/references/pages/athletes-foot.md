# Athlete's Foot

Athlete's foot (tinea pedis) is a common fungal infection that affects the feet. Our team can assess and help manage this condition.

**What We Offer:**
- Assessment of suspicious skin changes
- Education on prevention and self-care
- Recommendations for over-the-counter and prescription treatments
- Referral to Dr. Nesbitt if needed

**Prevention Tips:**
- Keep feet clean and dry
- Wear moisture-wicking socks
- Change socks regularly
- Use antifungal powder in shoes
- Avoid walking barefoot in public areas
