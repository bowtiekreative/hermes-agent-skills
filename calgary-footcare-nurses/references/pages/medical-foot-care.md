# Medical Foot Care

Our comprehensive medical foot care services include professional assessment and treatment by qualified nursing staff. Each appointment provides meticulous attention to your foot health needs.

**Services Include:**
- Assessment of foot and lower extremity health
- Nail care and trimming
- Callus and corn removal
- Ingrown nail management
- Diabetic foot screening
- Neuropathy assessment
- Skin and nail assessment
- Education on proper foot care

**Recommended Frequency:** Every 6-8 weeks for optimal foot health.

**Doctor Availability:** Dr. Nesbitt works on Tuesdays and Thursdays — you can have your medical foot care done by one of our wonderful nurses and have a check-up done by the doctor on the same day.
