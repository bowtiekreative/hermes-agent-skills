# Plantar Fasciitis

Plantar fasciitis causes heel pain and can significantly impact your daily activities. Our team can help assess, manage, and treat this condition.

**Our Approach:**
- Comprehensive assessment of foot mechanics
- Orthotic assessment and prescription through Onward Orthotics
- Education on stretching and self-management
- Coordination with Dr. Nesbitt if medical intervention is needed

**Symptoms We Treat:**
- Heel pain, especially with first steps in the morning
- Arch pain
- Pain after prolonged standing
- Heel spurs (often associated with plantar fasciitis)

Orthotic solutions from our Certified Pedorthist can provide significant relief for plantar fasciitis.
