# Heel Spurs

A heel spur is a calcium deposit causing a bony protrusion on the underside of the heel bone. They are often associated with plantar fasciitis.

**Our Approach:**
- Comprehensive assessment
- Pain management strategies
- Orthotic solutions through Onward Orthotics
- Education on stretching and self-management
- Referral for medical evaluation if needed

While heel spurs themselves may not cause pain, the associated inflammation and soft tissue irritation can be managed effectively with proper care.
