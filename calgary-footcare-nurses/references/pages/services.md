# Services

Calgary Foot Care Nurses offers comprehensive foot care services both in-clinic and through mobile visits.

## Medical Foot Care

- Medical foot care at each appointment
- Lower extremity assessments
- Callus and corn removal
- Ingrown nail cleanup
- Diabetic foot care & neuropathy assessment
- Leg and foot assessments (mobile and in-clinic)

[Full details →](medical-foot-care.md)

## Conditions We Treat

| Condition | Treatment |
|-----------|-----------|
| Diabetic Foot Care | Regular monitoring, neuropathy assessment, wound prevention |
| Neuropathy | Assessment and management of peripheral nervous system conditions |
| Peripheral Vascular Disease | Lower extremity assessment and education |
| Ingrown Nails | Cleanup by nurse; removal by doctor if needed |
| Calluses & Corns | Removal by qualified nursing staff |
| Plantar Fasciitis | Assessment and management |
| Bunions | Assessment and management |
| Hammertoes | Assessment and management |
| Heel Spurs | Assessment and management |
| Athlete's Foot | Treatment and management |
| Ulcers | Venous, diabetic, arterial, and post-traumatic wound management |
| Warts | Treatment options available |

## Partners & Products

### Onward Orthotics
Custom orthotics and footwear solutions through Heather Orosz, Canadian Certified Pedorthist.

### Sigvaris Compression Socks
Range from breathable cotton/linen to athletic compression stockings (8-15 mmHg to 50-60 mmHg). Visit clinic for proper sizing.

### Juzo Compression Therapy Garments
Latex-free, Lycra-based compression garments. Unique inlay thread technique ensures consistent compression. FiberSoft® process for comfort. Machine-washable.

### NanoTess
Social enterprise using nanotechnology innovation for chronic wounds (diabetic foot ulcers). Combines nursing expertise with NanoTess technology.
