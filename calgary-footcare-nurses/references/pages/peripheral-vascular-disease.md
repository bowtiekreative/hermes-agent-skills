# Peripheral Vascular Disease (PVD)

Peripheral Vascular Disease affects blood flow in the lower extremities. Our team provides comprehensive assessment and management for patients with PVD.

**Our Services:**
- Lower extremity assessment
- Ankle brachial index (ABI) testing
- Skin and nail assessment for high-risk patients
- Education on PVD management
- Coordinated care with Dr. Nesbitt
- Compression therapy when appropriate
- Regular monitoring and follow-up

We emphasize education and awareness to help patients manage their condition and prevent complications.
