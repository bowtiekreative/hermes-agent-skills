# Simple Dressings

We provide simple dressing services for foot wounds and post-procedure care.

**Services Include:**
- Wound cleaning and dressing
- Post-procedure care (after ingrown nail removal, etc.)
- Monitoring of healing progress
- Education on home care

For complex or chronic wounds, Dr. Nesbitt provides comprehensive ulcer management including compressive therapy and advanced wound care.
