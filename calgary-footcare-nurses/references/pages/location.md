## Directions

**From Deerfoot Trail:**
- Exit Glenmore west
- North on Macleod Trail past Chinook Mall
- Left on 58th Ave
- Second right, park in the back of the building for use of the elevator
- Elevator located in the middle of the building
- Buzzer is used to get into building — please call (825) 540-5888 press 1 to ask receptionist for buzzer code

## Accessibility

- Free parking in both the back and front of the building
- Elevator access is in the back of the building
- Stairs are in the front of the building
- Access via Calgary Transit Chinook train station
- Bus routes: #10, 148, 36, 43
