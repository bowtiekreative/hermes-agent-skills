# Callus & Corn Removal

Calluses and corns can be uncomfortable and, if left untreated, can lead to more serious problems, especially for diabetic patients. Our nursing team provides professional removal.

**Our Service:**
- Professional callus removal by trained nursing staff
- Corn removal and management
- Assessment of underlying causes
- Education on prevention
- Regular maintenance (recommended every 6-8 weeks)

Calluses and corns are cleaned up during regular foot care appointments. For diabetic patients, regular removal is especially important to prevent skin breakdown and ulcers.
