# Frequently Asked Questions

## How many appointments can we book and how often should we be having medical foot care/check-ups done?

Recurring appointments are available all year. You can book up to 2 follow-ups in advance. We recommend foot care every **6-8 weeks**.

## What are the prices for Foot Care? Is there a charge to see the Doctor?

Please call (825) 540-5888 for current pricing. The **doctor visit is covered by Alberta Health Care** — there is no charge for Dr. Nesbitt's services.

## Can we get medical foot care done at the same time as we see Dr. Nesbitt?

**Yes.** Dr. Nesbitt works on Tuesdays and Thursdays. You can have your medical foot care done by one of our nurses and have a check-up done by the doctor on the same day.

## Do you know anything about nail fungus?

Nail fungus can be cleaned up during your regular foot care appointment. If needed, the doctor can treat the fungus promptly.

## Do you remove ingrown nails, calluses, and corns?

**Yes.** Ingrown nails, calluses, and corns are cleaned up and removed by our nurses during regular foot care appointments. For persistent ingrown nails, Dr. Nesbitt can perform removal procedures at the clinic.

## Do you have mobile services?

**Yes.** We offer home visits for foot and leg assessments and medical foot care. Mobile services are available Monday to Friday, 8:00am – 4:00pm.
