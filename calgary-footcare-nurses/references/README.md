# Calgary Foot Care Nurses

**Calgary Foot Care Nurses** specializes in diagnosing and treating conditions related to the peripheral nervous system, including Neuropathy, Peripheral Vascular Disease, Diabetes, calluses, corns, and ingrown nails. Our qualified team of nurses offers in-clinic care as well as mobile services to all of our patients.

We are committed to giving each patient meticulous attention and dedicated to improving health awareness and literacy amongst our patients.

**Direct Billing:** We offer direct billing for Alberta Blue Cross — no out-of-pocket payment required.

**Doctor:** Dr. John Nesbitt is available on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Doctor visits are covered by Alberta Health Care.
